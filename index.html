United States Housing Statistics (2015)

Background

This application visualizes geospatial data regarding 2015 housing statistics in the US. It displays a map of the US with states colored based on selected housing attributes.

Instructions

Users can hover with their mouse over either the map or the bar chart for information by state. The displayed attribute can be changed using the dropdown menu.

Sources
